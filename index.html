Dr. Chandrakanth Reddy Borra is actively engaged in research at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Data Management. His work addresses the critical role of databases in AI — including the management of structured and unstructured data, big data platforms, real-time streaming systems, and graph databases that support knowledge discovery and decision-making. His research interests further extend to the integration of cloud-based database systems with scalable AI applications.
